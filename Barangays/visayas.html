Visayas

Discover the beauty of Visayas in the Philippines.

Nature's Paradise

Visayas offers breathtaking views of lush landscapes and serene surroundings. It's a perfect escape for nature lovers.

  • Scenic trails for hiking and trekking.
  • Picturesque spots for photography enthusiasts.
  • Local delicacies and cultural experiences.

Visayas - Tourist Attractions

Explore the beauty of Visayas with these amazing attractions.

The Ruins (Negros Occidental)

This Western Visayas tourist spot used to be a luxurious architectural masterpiece and is often called the Taj Mahal of the Philippines.

Hinagdanan Cave (Bohol)

Hinagdanan is a limestone karst cave that was accidentally discovered by a local.

Biri Island (Samar)

Biri Island is one of the natural wonders of the Samar region.

Islas de Gigantes (Iloilo)

Islas de Gigantes is a group of islands situated off the coast of the northeastern Iloilo towns of Estancia and Carles.

Monad Shoal in Malapascua Island (Cebu)

If you are interested in diving in the Philippines then Monad Shoal makes an excellent tourist destination in Visayas
